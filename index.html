About Award

Travel Influencer Awards 23 is Ovigo's flagship award program under the mega campaign Dekhi Banglar Rup....

Travel Influencer Awards 23 is Ovigo's flagship award program under the mega campaign Dekhi Banglar Rup. The 'Travel Influencer Award' goes to those who influence people to explore beautiful Bangladesh and who work to develop the tourism and hospitality services of Bangladesh. This award will be given 60 awards in a total of 60 different categories under the prime 5 categories namely Creative Hero ( Travel Content), Travelers Icon (Influencer Travelers), Best in Service (Organization), Expert in Profession (Who serving with differently) and Special Contribution (Organization and personality who working behind the industry).

Who can Apply

If you are a traveller, explorer, or nomad who loves to go places, capture moments, and explore life through your stories and lenses this award is for you.
